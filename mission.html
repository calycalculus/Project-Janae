Mission Details

Your Mission

Agent NapkinFiend, We are deeply sorry to pull you from your day off, but only you can solve this case.

The mafia have long been a problem; however, recently they have outdone themselves. A rash of robberies and murders have occurred throughout Boise. A new laser technology, developed by our leading scientists at Area 52 , has been stolen and is being used by the mafia to commit these crimes.

Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to infiltrate the mafia and retrieve the laser technology.

Be careful, they are dangerous and will stop at nothing to keep their secrets safe.

To begin your mission, you must first find the location of the mafia's hideout. The first clue is hidden in this room. Use your detective skills to decipher the clue and find the location of the hideout. Good luck, Agent.

Objectives:

  1. Locate the mafia's hideout
  2. Retrieve the stolen laser technology
  3. Identify the key players in the mafia
  4. Bring the criminals to justice

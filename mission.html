Case 135

Agent Napkin, we are sorry to pull you from your day off. An urgent matter has come to our attention.

As you're aware, the Mafia has been quiet as of late. Lying low or, as we had hoped, gone for good. Sadly, as of today, they are officially back at it.
However, this time they have stolen a revolutionary and dangerous technology from our scientists at Area 50. This laser technology can kill a man without leaving any marks. The death appears to be a simple heart attack.
We are ashamed to say it took us almost a week to realize what had happened, due to a double-agent scientist who covered up the theft.
Now the Mafia has a weapon of terrifying significance, and they are using it to commit crimes all over the Boise area.
As the only agent team in Boise and our leading code breaker, you and your team are needed on this case ASAP.

In the envelope, you will find the last known possession of the previous agent on this case.
Agent 6 lost contact a month ago. All we recovered was this journal. According to Agent 6's personnel file, we believe the code is 606.

Good Luck, Agent!

Objectives:

  1. Locate the mafia's hideout
  2. Retrieve the stolen laser technology
  3. Identify the key players in the mafia
  4. Bring the criminals to justice

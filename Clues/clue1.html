Clue #1

At each crime scene, the Mafia has conveniently left behind a riddle. Each tends to take the form of a love poem or snippets of one; the reason why is unknown to us. Police at the scene found the latest clue on a discarded napkin.

Clue:

Every thought of you blooms soft in my mind,
Like something precious the world was meant to find.
A place dressed in color, in blushes and flame,
Where every bright flower whispers your name.
The air feels sweeter with each step taken near,
As though love itself has been carefully planted here.
Follow the petals wherever they grow—
My heart waits for you where the roses still glow.
